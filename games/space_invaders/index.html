Author:

Rahul Bethi




Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to shoot the aliens aproaching downwards, and dodge the bullets they shoot at you.
  • The barriers, when struck by both player bullets or enemy bullets gets damaged until completely destroyed.
  • The Player ship gets destroyedwhen it is struck by the enemy bullets.
  • The Player gets bonus points on shooting the Red Alien Space Ship on the top.
  • Game is Won by destroying all the enemy ships down.
  • Game is Over when player is out of lives or if the enemies reach the player.
  • Speed and frequency of enemy firing the bullets will increase as the enemies comes down.


How To Play:

  • The Player ship can be moved left and right using the respective 'Left' and 'Right' arrow keys.
  • Shoot bullets from the player ship using the 'Alt' key.


Logic Design and Implementation:

  • The whole game is constructed using the Bitmaps and square blocks
  • Player, enemies and enemy bullets have bitmaps, which change accordingly to show animation.
  • Square blocks are drawn in place of '1' and nothing in place of '0' in the bitmap.
  • Even the Score numbers are drawn using the bitmaps.
  • Barriers take few bullets to get completly damaged, thus protecting the player from enemy bullets.
  • Bullet class is created and the bullets are reused by activating and deactivationg them, making the game efficient.
  • Enemies movement logic is created along with their animation.
  • Speed is common for all enemies, and this speed is increased as the time goes on. so that enemies become faster and frequency at which they fire increases.
  • Bitmap of Barrier is altered to show damage.
